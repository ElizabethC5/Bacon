Bacon Recipes

Bacon Lettuce Tomato (BLT) Appetizer Bites
Toast bread, apply butter to bread (may cut off crust), cut bread into 4 or more pieces that are bite sized. Fry thick bacon, cut into bite-size pieces, tear Romaine lettuce into bite-size pieces, spear cherry tomato on toothpick with lettuce, bacon and bread. Yummy! (Healthy tip: use olive oil instead of butter, use turkey bacon, use whole-wheat bread.)

Brussel Sprouts with Bacon
After cleaning, cut fresh brussel sprouts in half if small or in 3 pieces. Chop bacon before frying, fry bacon with a small amount of olive oil until crisp. Add brussel sprouts to pan on medium heat, stir, cover for 3-5min, season with salt and pepper, simmer on low for 5-10 minutes, stir every so often. This is good as a side dish.

Corn Salad with Pancetta
Clean corn salad (also called Rapunzel salad or in German "Feldsalat"). Chop walnuts or pecans. Chop and fry red onion. Fry pancetta until beginning to brown, add chopped red onion. Drain and reserve the fat. Mix corn salad with onion, pancetta and nuts, May add cranberries or blood orange pieces. Mix balsamic vinegar with the pancetta fat with the proportion of 1/3 vinegar to 2/3 fat. Drizzle over salad and mix. Heavenly! Serve with Riesling white wine. (Even healthier tip, substitute extra virgin olive oil for the pancetta fat.)
